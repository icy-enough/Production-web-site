UX Research

UX Research, or User Experience Research, is a systematic process of study and analysis focused on understanding the needs, behaviors and motivations of the users of a product or service.

UX Design

UX Design, or User Experience Design, is a discipline that focuses on creating products, services or systems that provide meaningful and relevant experiences for users.

UI Design

UI Design, or User Interface Design, is a crucial discipline in digital product development. It is the process of designing visual interfaces in software and applications, with a focus on appearance and interactive style. Creating attractive, intuitive and efficient interfaces that facilitate user interaction with the digital product.

UX Audit

A UX Audit (user experience audit) is a detailed evaluation of the user experience of a digital product, such as a website, mobile application or software. The objective is to identify issues and opportunities for improvement in terms of usability, accessibility, performance and user satisfaction.
